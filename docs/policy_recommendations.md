# Policy Recommendations

> Status: framework only, pending real evidence (see `evidence_synthesis.md`).
> **Scope note:** "policy" here means organizational/deployment policy
> (what a team building on LLMs should do), not government regulation or
> legislative positions — see `problem_statement.md`, "What this project
> is explicitly not."

## Intended structure and tone

Every recommendation in this section, once written, will follow the
pattern:

> "\[Benchmark\] observed \[specific measured result, with citation to
> evidence_synthesis.md\]. Organizations in \[specific deployment context\]
> may want to consider \[specific, actionable practice\], because
> \[direct link back to the observed result\]."

Not:

> "Companies should improve X" (no evidence link)
> "Governments should require Y" (wrong scope for this evidence base)

If a draft recommendation in this section can't be traced to a specific
row in `evidence_synthesis.md`, it doesn't belong here yet.
