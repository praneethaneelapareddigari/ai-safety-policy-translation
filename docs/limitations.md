# Limitations

- **Evidence base is currently pending.** As of this scaffold, neither
  sibling benchmark has contributed real (non-mock, non-pilot-caveated)
  evidence yet. See `evidence_synthesis.md` status banner.
- **Two benchmarks, narrow scope.** Even once populated, this project
  synthesizes findings from two specific studies (multilingual refusal
  consistency; prompt-injection/jailbreak guardrail comparison), not a
  comprehensive survey of LLM safety literature. Claims here are scoped
  to "what these two benchmarks found," not "what is generally true about
  LLM safety."
- **Not peer-reviewed.** This is a portfolio synthesis project, not a
  submission to a venue with independent review. Treat conclusions
  accordingly.
- **Single-author, single-environment experiments.** No independent
  replication of either benchmark's findings exists yet.
- **Not a substitute for domain-specific regulatory analysis.**
  Organizational deployment guidance here does not constitute legal or
  compliance advice for any specific jurisdiction.
